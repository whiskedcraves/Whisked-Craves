Deposit Policy

Our Terms

Every order requires a non-refundable deposit. This is not about distrust — it is about respect for the time, ingredients and care that go into every single box. Please only claim if you are genuinely intending to collect.

Drop Boxes (4, 6 & 12)
Deposit required within the timeframe stated in our confirmation message. Your box is not held until the deposit is received. No refunds on deposit.
Large Orders (32+)
50% non-refundable deposit required to book your date. Balance paid on collection. Minimum 7–10 days lead time required.
Non-collection — If you fail to collect after paying a deposit, the deposit is not returned. If circumstances change, please contact us as early as possible.
